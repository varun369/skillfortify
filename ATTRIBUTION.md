## Attribution in Outputs

SkillFortify embeds three layers of attribution in its outputs:

1. **Visible Attribution** — Human-readable footer in reports and CLI output.
2. **Cryptographic Signing** — SHA-256 content hash with tool metadata in JSON outputs.
3. **Steganographic Watermarking** — Invisible zero-width Unicode markers in text outputs.

## Verification

To verify a SkillFortify output:

